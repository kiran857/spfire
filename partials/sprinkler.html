SPRINKLER SYSTEM :


STANDARD RESPONSE SPRINKLERS:-

 

It is UL Listed & FM approved.It uses the glass bulb containing special liquid instead of conventional fuse, ensuring stable operation against varying ambient temperature.

 



SIDE WALL TYPE SPRINKLERS:-

 

It is UL Listed & FM Approved. It is available in both horizontal and vertical type. It uses the glass bulb containing special liquid instead of conventional fuse, ensuring stable operation against varying ambient temperature.

CONCEALED TYPE SPRINKLERS:-

 

It is UL Listed & FM Approved.It absorbs heat through cover plate secured to the fusible alloy.At rated temperature,cover plate falls away exposing sprinkler to direct heat causing deflector to drop, allowing water spraying operation. 

UPRIGHT SPRINKLERS:-

 

It is UL Listed & FM Approved. Its K factor is 11.2(160)/14(200)/ 17(242), threaded size is NPT-3/4”,Temperature Ratings are 68, 74,93, 100 and 141 degree Celsius. Its orifice size is 3/4” and finish is Bronze.

N252, PENDENT TYPE SPRINKLERS:-

 

It is UL Listed & FM Approved. Its K factor is 25.2 (362), thread size is NPT-1” and Temperature ratings are 74 and 100 degree Celsius. Its orifice size is 1”.

PENDENT SPRINKLERS:-

 

Its UL Listed & FM Approved. Its K factor is 5.6 (80)/ 8 (115)/k-11.2(160), threaded size is NPT-1/2” & 3/4” and temperature ratings are 57, 68,79,93,74 and 100 degree Celsius. Orifice is ½” & ¾”. 

EXTRA LARGE ORIFICE SPRINKLER:-

 

It is UL Listed & FM Approved. Its K factor is 11.2(160), thread size is NPT-¾” and temperature ratings are 74, 100 & 141 degree Celsius.Its orifice size is 3/4”. 

MEDIUM VELOCITY WATER SPRAY SYSTEM(M.V.W.S.):-

 

It is UL Listed & FM Approved. Its K factor is 1.9(28), 3.05(50), 5.8(85), its thread size is NPT-1/2” and discharge angles are 80, 110 & 120 degree. Its nozzle diameters are 0.257”,0.328” & 0.453”.
